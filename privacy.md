Privacy Policy (DryRun)

Effective Date: 04-30-2026

DryRun (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the DryRun mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

DryRun is designed to work without collecting personal data.

At this time:

We do not require account creation
We do not collect personal information
We do not track your activity across apps or websites

All data you create in the app (such as routines, notes, and cues) is stored locally on your device.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to:

Allow you to build and rehearse routines
Store your content for use within the app

We do not access, analyze, or transmit your data to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device.

If you delete the app, your data may be permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

DryRun does not currently use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

If this changes in the future (for example, adding integrations or analytics), this policy will be updated.

6. Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device’s security settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

DryRun does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Updates will be reflected by a revised “Effective Date.”
